    中江岩戸神楽｜十二番｜降臨（ごうりん）｜七人立
    天照大御神から三種の神器を授かった皇孫天津彦火瓊々杵尊が、諸神を引き連れ猿田彦大神の先導で、日向の高千穂の峯に降りられる形の舞である。 （和装本　中江岩戸神楽　編集発行 波野村教育委員会より抜粋）

    Nakae Iwato Kagura, No. 12, Gorin (Seven-person performance)
    This sacred dance depicts Ninigi-no-Mikoto, the grandson of Amaterasu Omikami, who received the Three Sacred Treasures and descended to the peak of Takachiho in Hyuga, accompanied by various deities and guided by Sarutahiko Okami. A Kagura performance depicting the Tenson Korin, the heavenly descent of Ninigi-no-Mikoto in Japanese mythology.
